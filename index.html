Jedi Unlock Guide

The classic pre-Publish 9 Hologrind path — rebuilt for SWGReturns.

01

Holocron Unlock

  • Holocrons reveal the 6 professions you must master to unlock Jedi
  • Each Holocron gives one profession – you must master all 6
  • Once all 6 are mastered, your existing character becomes a Jedi
  • You keep all your skills, stats, and progress — you are now a Jedi
  • One Jedi character per account
02

Knight Trials

After unlocking Jedi, you must complete the following Knight Trials to advance beyond Padawan:

  • Tusken Raider – Hunt 10 Tusken Raiders
  • Ancient Bull Rancor – Hunt 1 Ancient Bull Rancor
  • Stintaril Prowler – Hunt 4 Stintaril Prowlers
  • Blurrg Raptor – Hunt 3 Blurrg Raptors
  • Enraged Kimogila – Hunt 1 Enraged Kimogila
  • Peko Peko Albatross – Hunt 2 Peko Peko Albatrosses
  • Graul Marauder – Hunt 2 Graul Marauders
  • Enemy Soldier – Hunt 8 Enemy Soldiers (Rebel: Storm Commando / Imperial: Rebel Commando)
  • High General – Hunt 4 High Generals (Rebel: Imperial High/General / Imperial: Rebel High/General)
  • Nightsister Protector – Hunt 2 Nightsister Protectors
  • Giant Canyon Krayt Dragon – Hunt 1 Giant Canyon Krayt Dragon
  • Light or Dark – Choose your path before the Jedi Council
03

Force Ranking System

  • After completing the Knight Trials and reaching Jedi Master, enter the Force Ranking System
  • Choose the Light Side or Dark Side path
  • Compete for rank, power, and prestige
  • Earn powerful rewards and unique progression
04

Jedi Elder

  • The ultimate stage of Jedi progression
  • Reserved for the most dedicated Force users
Important: Your existing character becomes Jedi after mastering the 6 Holocron professions – no separate slot or new character needed. Only one Jedi per account. No Jedi death penalty. FRS progression begins after Jedi Knight.
