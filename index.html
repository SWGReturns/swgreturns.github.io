Server Features

Designed from the ground up to preserve the spirit of early Star Wars Galaxies.

```

Core Gameplay

  • Authentic Pre-CU combat and profession systems
  • No NGE mechanics or simplification
  • Balanced enhancements for long-term play

Jedi System

  • Pre-Publish 9 Jedi implementation
  • Holocron-based unlock (4 professions)
  • Jedi remain rare and meaningful
  • Progression designed for commitment, not speed

Progression & XP

  • Enhanced XP rates (not instant leveling)
  • Long-term progression curve
  • Endgame content focused on mastery

Species & Customization

  • Extended playable species (45+)
  • Expanded cosmetic and appearance options
  • Species diversity without balance breaks

Custom Professions

  • Basebuster profession
  • Additional custom professions
  • Designed to complement Pre-CU balance
```

What We Are / What We Aren’t

```

What We Are

  • Hardcore Pre-CU focused
  • Progression-driven, not instant gratification
  • Jedi as a long-term achievement
  • Community-first and stability-focused

What We Aren’t

  • Not an NGE or CU server
  • No instant Jedi or handouts
  • No theme-park shortcuts
  • No pay-to-win mechanics
```
