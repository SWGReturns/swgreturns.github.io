Getting Started

1️⃣ Download the Launcher

Download and install the SWGReturns launcher. It handles patching and server connection.

Download Launcher

You’ll need a legal SWG client install. The launcher handles all server-side patching.

2️⃣ Auto-Register In-Game

No website or Discord registration required. Your account is created automatically the first time you log in.

  • Open the launcher
  • Enter a new username and password
  • Click Login
  • Your account is auto-registered on first login

Passwords are securely hashed. Staff cannot view your credentials.
