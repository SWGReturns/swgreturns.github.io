Features

Two galaxies, one community — each server offers a unique Star Wars Galaxies experience.

Pre-CU Classic

The original sandbox — profession-based skill system, open world PvP, and the classic Hologrind Jedi path. 10× XP, expanded planets, and custom loot make it the definitive Pre-CU experience.

  • Classic pre-Publish 9 Hologrind Jedi unlock
  • Padawan → Journeyman → Master → Elder progression
  • Force Ranking System (FRS) endgame
  • No death penalty on Jedi status
  • 5th generation lightsabers supported
  • Custom loot: Revan's Robes, SWTOR Robes, Star Forge Robes
  • 9+ additional planets beyond the original galaxy
  • 10× XP solo / 4× XP grouped
  • 200 item cap, improved slicing, junk dealers accept all

NGE — Jump to Lightspeed

The full NGE experience with space combat, class-based progression, and all the polish of the后期的游戏. Active community with custom content and a dedicated test center.

  • Jump to Lightspeed space combat expansion
  • Class-based combat system with all NGE classes
  • Full space gameplay — pilot starships, dogfight in orbit
  • Active test center with a friendly frog in the Taanab cantina
  • Custom content additions and balance improvements
  • Stable server with active development
  • Regular events and community gatherings
  • Dedicated staff keeping the galaxy alive
