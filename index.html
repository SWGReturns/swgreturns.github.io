Full Jedi Progression System

  • 🔵 Padawan Unlock: Customized Jedi Holocron unlock, player consumes 10 Holocrons to Padawan, NOT your typical profession(s) or standard village grind!
  • Holocron radial: “Use for Studies” + “Speak to the Gatekeeper” (unlocks when threshold is met)
  • Holocron speaks to the player → gives final test → unlocks Padawan
  • Holocrons are consumed on “Speak to Gatekeeper” use
  • 🟡 Jedi Knight Unlock: Master 3 Jedi/FS skill trees (e.g. Lightsaber, Defender, Force Healing)
  • After 3 trees: point accumulation resumes with atmospheric progression messages
  • Consume 50 holocrons and kill 4 dark jedi npcs (via radial “Use for Studies”)
  • Bring specific items to your chosen enclave (Light or Dark) — alignment chosen by enclave
  • 🔴 Jedi Master / Dark Lord Unlock: Consume 150 holocrons + kill Revan, group is recommended

🌟 JEDI UNLOCK GUIDE (SWG Returns – Holocron System)

🔵 Padawan Unlock

  • No village, no profession grind. Use 10 Holocrons to unlock Padawan.
  • Holocron radial menu:
    • Use for Studies – consume for progress.
    • Speak to the Gatekeeper – appears when threshold is met.
  • Holocron speaks to you → gives final test → unlocks Padawan.
  • Holocrons are consumed only when using Speak to the Gatekeeper.

🟡 Jedi Knight Unlock

  • Master 3 Jedi / Force Sensitive skill trees (e.g. Lightsaber, Defender, Force Healing).
  • After 3 trees, point accumulation resumes with atmospheric progression messages.
  • Consume 50 Holocrons (via Use for Studies).
  • Kill 4 Dark Jedi NPCs.
  • Bring specific items to your chosen enclave (Light or Dark) – alignment is chosen by the enclave.

🔴 Jedi Master / Dark Lord Unlock

  • Consume 150 Holocrons.
  • Kill Revan (group recommended).

⚖️ Grey Jedi

  • Serve both sides of the Force.
  • Unlocks extra Force abilities beyond Light/Dark restrictions.
  • Weaker than pure Light or Dark, but more versatile.

✨ Grandmaster (Light & Dark)

  • Endgame progression for Light Side and Dark Side.
  • Long‑term prestige path.

⚠️ Important
– All Jedi progress is permanent – no death penalty.
– Knight trials are at the Yavin 4 enclave (alignment chosen there).
– Use /checkforcestatus to track your progress (admins are alerted).
