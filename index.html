🌟 JEDI UNLOCK GUIDE (Pre-CU)

🔵 Force Sensitive Character Slot (FSCS)

  • SWGReturns uses a classic Hologrind-inspired Jedi system.
  • Holocrons reveal hidden professions required for Jedi unlock.
  • Master all required professions to unlock a Force Sensitive Character Slot.
  • Your existing character does NOT become Jedi.
  • Log out and create a brand-new Jedi character after unlocking.
  • One Jedi character is permitted per account.

🟡 Starting Your Jedi Journey

  • New Jedi characters begin as Jedi Padawan.
  • Train Lightsaber and Force abilities.
  • Craft and customize your own lightsabers.
  • Advance through Padawan, Journeyman, and Master ranks.

⚔️ Force Ranking System (FRS)

  • After reaching Jedi Master, enter the Force Ranking System.
  • Choose the Light Side or Dark Side path.
  • Compete for rank, power, and prestige.
  • Earn powerful rewards and unique progression.

✨ Jedi Elder

  • The ultimate stage of Jedi progression.
  • Reserved for the most dedicated Force users.

⚠️ Important
• Jedi unlocks are account-based.
• Only one Jedi per account is allowed.
• No Jedi death penalty.
• Hologrind unlocks a new character slot—not Jedi on your current character.
• FRS progression begins after Jedi Knight.
