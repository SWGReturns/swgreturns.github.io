Guides

Your complete path to Jedi — from unlock to Knight Trials.

Jedi Unlock Guide

The classic pre-Publish 9 Hologrind path — rebuilt for SWGReturns.

01

Holocron Unlock

  • Holocrons reveal the 6 professions you must master to unlock Jedi.
  • Each Holocron gives one profession – you must master all 6.
  • Once all 6 are mastered, your existing character becomes a Jedi.
  • You keep all your skills, stats, and progress — you are now a Jedi.
  • One Jedi per account.
02

Starting Your Jedi Journey

  • New Jedi characters begin as Jedi Padawan.
  • Train Lightsaber and Force abilities.
  • Craft and customize your own lightsabers.
  • Advance through Padawan, Journeyman, and Master ranks.
03

Force Ranking System

  • After reaching Jedi Master, enter the Force Ranking System.
  • Choose the Light Side or Dark Side path.
  • Compete for rank, power, and prestige.
  • Earn powerful rewards and unique progression.
04

Jedi Elder

  • The ultimate stage of Jedi progression.
  • Reserved for the most dedicated Force users.
Important: Your existing character becomes Jedi after mastering the 6 Holocron professions – no separate slot or new character needed. Only one Jedi per account. No Jedi death penalty. FRS progression begins after Jedi Knight.

May the Force be with you.

Knight Trials Guide

Complete all 12 trials to earn your Jedi Knight rank.

If you've made it this far, that means you have completed your Holocron unlocks, your Padawan training, and have trained in the Jedi ways. Now comes the next step — The Knight Trials.

The Knight Trials are moderately difficult and can take a while to finish. Most of the creatures mentioned only reveal themselves after the lair they spawn from has been destroyed — keep in mind that they do have a low chance to show up.

01

Kill 10 Tusken Raiders

  • Location: Tatooine
  • Best spots: Fort Tusken (Tusken Outpost shuttle), Jawa vs. Tusken Battlefield (/way 5778 3982), or near Jabba's Palace.
02

Kill 1 Ancient Bull Rancor

  • Location: Dathomir
  • Static spawn at Mutant Rancor Cave entrance (/way -4225 -2085) and near the Imperial Prison (South/Southwest).
03

Kill 4 Stintaril Prowlers

  • Location: Yavin IV
  • Pull Giant Stintaril missions and destroy the lair. Found between Labor Outpost and Geonosian Cave, or Northwest of the Labor Outpost.
04

Kill 3 Blurrg Raptors

  • Location: Endor
  • Pull Blurrg Hunter missions (solo, CL 50-57) and destroy the lair. Found around the center of the map, near Smuggler's Outpost, and near the Deathwatch Bunker (Northwest).
05

Kill 1 Enraged Kimogila

  • Location: Lok
  • Pull Kimogila missions (group, CL 95+) and destroy the lair. They only spawn from these missions.
06

Kill 2 Peko Peko Albatrosses

  • Location: Naboo
  • Pull Giant Peko Peko missions and destroy the lair. Found 1500-3000m from Highgarden, Kaadara, or Keren.
07

Kill 2 Graul Marauders

  • Location: Dantooine
  • Pull Frenzied Graul missions (high-level group) and destroy the lair. Found on the Western side of the map or near the Force Crystal Hunter's Cave.
08

Choose Your Faction — Light or Dark Side

  • Earn at least 200 faction points in either the Rebellion or Imperial forces.
  • This decision can be undone only by restarting your Knight Trials.
  • You will not be made permanently overt until after you finish the Knight Trials.
09

Kill 8 Enemy Soldiers

  • Imperial: Kill 8 Rebel Commandos (Yavin IV /way 1591 1536, Rori Rebel Outpost, or Dantooine Rebel Outpost).
  • Rebel: Kill 8 Stormtrooper Commandos (Bela Vistal on Corellia /way 6936 -5538, or Lok Imperial Outpost).
10

Kill 4 High Generals

  • Imperial: Kill Rebel High Generals / Generals.
  • Rebel: Kill Imperial High Generals / Generals.
  • Locations (respawn 6-20 min):
  • Weapons Depot Facility (Talus) — 6 Surface Marshals
  • Rori Hyperdrive Facility — 6 Surface Marshals / High Generals
  • Stronghold POI (Corellia) — 2 Surface Marshals
  • Lok Imperial Outpost — 1 Imperial Surface Marshal
  • Rori Rebel Outpost — 1 Rebel High General + 1 Surface Marshal
11

Kill 2 Nightsister Protectors

  • Location: Nightsister Stronghold, Dathomir (POI) /way 3946 -50
  • Static spawns, 20-minute respawn timer.
12

Kill 1 Giant Canyon Krayt Dragon

  • Location: Tatooine
  • Krayt Graveyard (POI) /way 6839 4370 or Ancient Krayt Dragon Skeleton /way -4632 -4341.
  • Upon killing it, you will automatically receive the title of Jedi Knight or Dark Jedi Knight along with corresponding faction robes. You are now permanently overt and eligible for the Force Ranking System.
Good luck out there! The Force will be with you, always.
