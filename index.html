SWGReturns Pre-CU Patch Notes Live

Released: October 29, 2025
  • Holocron Unlock: 5 random professions to unlock, NO need to do the padawan trials.
  • Travel: FS Village, and many other locations added to ticket terminals.
  • XP: increased EXP gain — 10× solo and 12x in groups. Spin groups are enabled; XP is enhanced in groups too.
  • Missions: take up to 6 missions at a time.
  • Balance: Jedi are an alpha class.
  • Jedi: Pre-publish 9 Jedi + FRS enabled + 5th-gen sabers and other perks (e.g., custom FRS skills available).
  • Vendor: Custom vendor added for buffs, armor, weapons, and other goodies outside major starports and cities, etc.
  • Planet additions: dromund kaas and taanab have been added as planets, with 9 more to come.
  • Starting jedi: jedi is available as a option on creation.
