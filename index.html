Auto-Register In-Game

No web or Discord registration needed. Your account is created automatically in-game the first time you log in.

  1. Open the launcher
  2. Enter a new username and password
  3. Click Login
  4. Your account will be created automatically, then you’ll enter the galaxy

Your password is securely stored (hashed). Staff cannot view your password.

Start the launcher, log in with your credentials, and enter the galaxy.
