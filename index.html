Jedi Unlock Guide

The complete path from fresh character to Jedi Knight on SWG Returns.

⭐ Step 1 — Get Glowy

Earn the Force Sensitive glow by collecting badges:

  • 1 profession badge
  • 1 content badge
  • 3 Jedi POI badges
  • 2 difficult POI badges
  • 5 easy POI badges

Use /checkforcestatus to track your progress (note: admins are alerted when used).

🌿 Step 2 — Find the Village

  • Once glowy, head 1,000m outside a major city (e.g. Theed)
  • Wait 5–10 minutes for the Old Man to spawn and talk to him
  • Wait another 5–10 minutes then kill the Sith spawns — loot the datapad for a waypoint
  • Follow the waypoint, kill the Sith camp, loot that datapad for the Village waypoint
  • Travel to the Village on Dathomir via the Dathomir Science Outpost
  • Complete a maximum of 3 Force Sensitive trees in full — village phases are 24 hours each, one phase per day
  • Full village phase guide: SWGEmu Village Guide
  • After completing your 3 trees, the Old Man returns in 5–10 minutes with a waypoint to Mellichae's camp
  • Mellichae fight is brutal — group up!

🔵 Step 3 — Padawan Trials

  • After Mellichae, your Padawan Trials begin
  • Trial 1 and 3 are random per character
  • Trial 2 is always constructing your training lightsaber
  • Start by meditating at a Force Shrine (e.g. near Bela Vistal shuttleport)
  • Complete all trials, then kneel and meditate again to receive Padawan status
  • Begin grinding your pre-9 Jedi skills

🟡 Step 4 — Knight Trials (Yavin 4)

  • After reaching Jedi Knight in your pre-9 Jedi skills, you'll be prompted to begin your Knight Trials
  • Three trials in total - kill 25 tuskan raiders, choose your alignment, and kill 15 rebel commandos or imperial troopers.
  • Light or Dark alignment is chosen here — this is permanent
  • Buff up and stock stims before starting

📋 Key Tips & FAQ

  • ✅ All Jedi progress is permanent — no loss on death
  • ✅ You keep all your non-Jedi professions
  • ✅ Light vs Dark choice is permanent — choose wisely
  • ✅ You cannot be both Light and Dark
  • ⚠️ You need all 250 skill points — plan your builds
  • ⚠️ Mellichae can be soloed but groups are strongly recommended
  • ⏱️ Estimated time from unlock to Knight: 10–20 hours
  • 💬 Command: /checkforcestatus — shows your Force progress

⚠️ Important
– All Jedi progress is permanent – no death penalty.
– Knight trials are at the Yavin 4 enclave (alignment chosen there).
