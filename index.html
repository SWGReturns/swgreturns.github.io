Getting Started

1️⃣ Download the Launcher

Download and install the SWGReturns launcher. It handles patching and server connection.

Download Launcher

You’ll need a legal SWG client install. The launcher handles server patching.

2️⃣ Auto-Register In-Game

No Discord or website registration required. Your account is created automatically the first time you log in.

  • Open the launcher
  • Enter a new username and password
  • Click Login
  • Your account will auto-register on first login

Passwords are securely hashed. Staff cannot view your password.

3️⃣ Launch & Play

Start the launcher, log in, and enter the galaxy.

  • Patch completes automatically
  • Select your character
  • Head planetside
