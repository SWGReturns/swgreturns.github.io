Jedi Unlock Guide

The complete path from fresh character to Jedi Knight on SWG Returns – classic holocron unlock, no Village.

🗝️ Phase 1 – Unlock Jedi Padawan (2 Professions)

  • Step 1: Master any non‑force profession (Novice → Master).
  • Step 2: Use a Holocron – it reveals the second profession you need.
  • Step 3: Master that second profession.
  • Step 4: Use another Holocron → you are granted Padawan status.

📦 Holocrons drop from high‑level mobs (NS Elders, Krayt Dragons) and can be bought from vendors.

⚔️ Phase 2 – Knight Trials (3 Trials)

  • After reaching Padawan, speak to the Gatekeeper (ask in Discord for location).
  • Trial 1 – Tusken Slayer
    Kill 25 Tusken Raiders (anywhere on Tatooine).
  • Trial 2 – Choose Your Path
    A popup window appears – pick Light Side or Dark Side.
    ⚠️ This choice is permanent – cannot be changed later!
  • Trial 3 – Faction Hunt
    Kill 15 enemy faction members:
    → If you chose Light: kill 15 Imperials
    → If you chose Dark: kill 15 Rebels
    (Faction NPCs can be found in PvP zones like Restuss on Rori)
  • After Trial 3, you become a Jedi Knight and receive your alignment robe + force_rank_light_novice / force_rank_dark_novice.

💪 Buff up and bring stims – each trial may be repeated after a short cooldown if you fail.

✨ Phase 3 – Jedi Master (Post‑Knight)

  • After Knight, earn GCW Skill XP from PvP kills.
  • Use the Enclave terminal (Light Enclave on Yavin 4 for Light, Dark Enclave for Dark) to purchase FRS ranks.
  • Reach FRS Rank 11 to become Grand Master (Light) or Dark Lord (Dark).

📋 Key Tips & FAQ

  • ✅ All Jedi progress is permanent — no loss on death
  • ✅ You keep all your non-Jedi professions
  • ✅ Light vs Dark choice is permanent — choose wisely
  • ✅ You cannot be both Light and Dark
  • ⚠️ You need all 250 skill points — plan your builds
  • ⏱️ Estimated time from unlock to Knight: 10–20 hours
  • 💬 Command: /checkforcestatus — shows your Force progress
