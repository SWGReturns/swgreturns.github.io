Two Ways to Play

Core3 Pre-CU

Classic sandbox progression, profession-based builds, Jedi village unlock, player economy, custom loot, and expanded planets.

NGE JTL

Jump to Lightspeed, class-based combat, space gameplay, expanded playable species, events, and active development.

Shared Community

Server features

Built for Returning Players

Core3 Main

  • Village Jedi Unlock: nine trials toward Knighthood.
  • Two Jedi Trees: Light Side and Dark Side progression.
  • Padawan, Knight, Master, and Elder progression paths.
  • Force Ranking System endgame.
  • Fifth generation lightsabers and expanded combat rewards.
  • Custom loot, rare armor sets, schematics, extra planets, improved slicing, and a 200 item cap.

NGE Main

  • Jump to Lightspeed space combat with pilot progression.
  • Expanded playable species including Chiss, Nautolan, Nightsister, and more.
  • Class-based NGE combat with all core professions.
  • Custom content additions, balance improvements, and regular events.
  • Dedicated staff keeping the galaxy active.

New to NGE? Head to Naboo - Theed and type /who in-game. Look for Jack Straw for help getting started.
