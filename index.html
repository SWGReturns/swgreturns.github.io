Getting Started

1️⃣ Download the Launcher

Download and install the SWGReturns launcher. It handles patching and server connection.

Download Launcher

2️⃣ Create Your Account (Discord)

Account registration is handled securely through Discord.

  1. Join our Discord
  2. Use /register
  3. Enter your username and password
  4. Launch the game and log in

Passwords are never visible to staff and are securely hashed.

Join Discord to Register

3️⃣ Launch & Play

Start the launcher, log in with your credentials, and enter the galaxy.
